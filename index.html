HOW TO PLAY

Movement: Use ZQSD (or WASD) to move.

Combat:

  • Pinch your index finger and thumb together in front of the camera to draw symbols on the screen.
  • Release the pinch to cast the spell matching the symbol (e.g., V for projectile, Circle for AoE, etc.).

Hub: Use your mouse to interact with the UI. Choose your spells at the preparation altar. You must equip a spell to cast it in the level.

Pause: Press [ESCAPE] at any time to pause the game.
